Easy Greek potato salad

Description

This dish is a especially ideal if you are on a diet and want to enjoy something which is both delicious and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 12 red potatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coarse salt, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried rosemary, crumbled
  • 1 pinch ground red pepper

Steps

  1. Place red potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water - (bring to a boil)
  2. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 25 minutes
  3. Drain and chill in freezer until cold, about 30 minutes
  4. Slice potatoes and place into a large salad bowl - toss potatoes with green onion
  5. Whisk olive oil, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, oregano, sugar, rosemary, and red pepper in a bowl
  6. Pour the dressing over the potatoes and toss
  7. Serve immediately or chill until serving time
