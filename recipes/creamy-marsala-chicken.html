Creamy Marsala Chicken

Description

This American Italian dish is one that will make you forget about your diet and indulge in a little bit of culinary sin.

We recommend you eat this delicious and simple dish with a plate of spaghetti next to it and a glass of white wine.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 cup sliced cremini mushrooms
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup Marsala wine

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper; cook in hot oil until browned, 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Stir onion and mushrooms with the chicken; continue cooking until the onion is translucent, 5 to 7 minutes.
  4. Pour cream of mushroom soup and Marsala wine over the chicken mixture;
  5. cook until hot and the chicken is no longer pink in the middle, 3 to 5 minutes.
